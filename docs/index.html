the heist

your personal art thief

Every day, the heist breaks into the world's great museums and steals one beautiful thing for your inbox. A masterpiece from the Met, the Art Institute of Chicago, Cleveland, the Smithsonian, Harvard, or SMK in Copenhagen. A line lifted from the canon. A find from the Library of Congress vaults. And a place to lay low.

No takeaways. No insights. No action items. It is a cleanser for an inbox full of business, and it asks nothing of you. Ninety seconds of beauty, then you get on with your day.
